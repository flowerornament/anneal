# Field Rollout Plan

The pilot covers three harbor stations, two storage sheds, and one coordinator
desk. Crews synchronize at the end of each day, but they must be able to make
local decisions while offline.

## Daily Flow

Route leads collect station observations, crate transfers, and exception
notes. Coordinators review the evidence graph the next morning.

## Risks

The main risk is a delayed station report that arrives after a crate transfer
has already been accepted. OQ-17 tracks that policy. OQ-19 tracks how long
temporary paper packet identifiers remain searchable during the pilot.
